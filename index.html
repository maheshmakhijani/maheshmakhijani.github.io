About Me

Hello! I'm a deep-tech product leader with a proven track record in building high-performing global teams, scaling silicon product organizations, and driving commercial success across edge AI and SoC development. Most recently, I led post-acquisition silicon product management at Snap, delivering multi-generational SoCs for smart glasses. Prior to that, I was instrumental in growing GrAI Matter Labs from an early prototype to a successful exit in the edge AI SoC space.

With a unique blend of product development, GTM strategy, and technical depth, I've built trusted partnerships within the silicon ecosystem, and worked across industries including AR, AI, consumer audio, and industrial automation.

When I'm not working with sand, you can find me exploring new technologies, mentoring young engineers, or enjoying a good cup of coffee while brainstorming the next big idea.
